# Data License & Usage Scope

This repository includes page images and text derived from:

**Stephen Return Riggs, _Dakota Grammar and Dictionary_ (c. 1890).**

To the best of our knowledge, this material is in the **public domain** due to age of publication. The code in this repository remains licensed under Apache-2.0 (see `LICENSE`).

**Scope & Respectful Use**
- Historical texts may contain outdated or harmful descriptions. Please use respectfully and in partnership with Dakota language experts and communities.
- If you believe a particular asset should be removed or annotated, please open an issue.
